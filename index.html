🧘 Pomodoro Technique Guide

How it works: Work in focused 25-minute sprints, then take a 5-minute break. After 4 sessions, take a 15-minute long break.

1
Close all distractions — phone on DND, close social media
2
Start timer. Work on ONE topic only for 25 minutes
3
Take 5-min break — stretch, water, walk around
4
After 4 sessions = 1 long break (15–20 min)
⚡
For SSC prep, use 50-10 pattern for complex topics (Quant/Reasoning): 50 min focused study + 10 min review notes.

💡 Anti-Procrastination Toolkit

When you don't feel like studying:

  • Use the 2-minute start rule — just open the book
  • Do the easiest topic first to build momentum
  • Set a 10-minute timer — "just 10 minutes"
  • Remember your WHY — visualize clearing Tier 1
  • Remove distractions — put phone in another room

Burnout Prevention:

  • Take 1 day off per 2 weeks if needed (buffer day)
  • Exercise 20 min daily — improves memory
  • Sleep 7–8 hours — non-negotiable for retention
  • Don't compare your progress with others
  • Celebrate small wins — every topic cleared matters
